# Gospel-Themed Youth Activity Ideas

Ideas for web-based gospel activities that could be built similar to the Walmart Photo Scavenger Hunt.

## 🔍 Scavenger Hunt Variations

| Activity | Concept | Gospel Twist |
|----------|---------|--------------|
| **Service Scavenger Hunt** | Find acts of service to do | "Inasmuch as ye have done it unto one of the least of these" |
| **Scripture Object Hunt** | Find items matching scripture stories | Connect physical objects to scripture stories |
| **Temple Prep Hunt** | Find items related to temple prep | Temples as house of learning, covenants |
| **Mission Prep Hunt** | Find mission prep items | "Preach My Gospel" principles |

## 📚 Scripture-Based Activities

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Scripture Mastery Game** | Match scriptures to topics, earn badges for mastery |
| **Scripture Story Sequencing** | Put stories in order, multiple difficulty levels |
| **Scripture Charades/Pictionary** | Act out or draw scripture stories for team guessing |
| **Chapter Check-in** | Track daily scripture reading with streaks and insights |
| **Scripture Meme Creator** | Create funny gospel memes to share |

## 🏆 Challenge & Competition

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **For Strength of Youth Challenge** | Track progress on each category with reflection prompts |
| **General Conference Bingo** | Bingo cards during conference, spot recurring themes |
| **Hymn Trivia Challenge** | Guess hymns from first lines, learn history |
| **Gospel Timeline Game** | Put church history events in chronological order |
| **Prophet Quote Match** | Match quotes to which prophet said them |

---

## 🌟 Top Picks for Web Apps

1. **Service Scavenger Hunt** - Like the Walmart Photo Scavenger Hunt but focused on finding acts of service
2. **For Strength of Youth Challenge** - 12-month tracking with challenges for each category
3. **Scripture Mastery Game** - Gamified scripture memorization with progress tracking
4. **Testimony Builder** - Guided prompts to help youth build and record their testimony over time
5. **Hymn Trivia Challenge** - Fun, educational, great for groups

---

## Notes

- All activities should:
  - Be mobile-friendly (phones are primary devices)
  - Work offline when possible (PWA)
  - Have simple, intuitive UI
  - Include progress tracking where applicable
  - Be shareable (groups, teams)
  - Focus on "finding gospel truths in everyday things"
